My life

Live in Warsaw but came from Szczecin. Every day I wake up with a smile on my face because here starts another part of my precious life. Positive and open to the world. Empathetic and present to people. I like company, but also value my own. Sometimes chatty and hyperactive, sometimes quiet and calm. I like music, sports and reflection.

My way

I started with a three-month bootcamp and I'm still learning on my own with freecodecamp, codecamedy, stackoverflow and udemy. It started to be bum rap with javascript. At first I don't succeed, then dust myself off and try again and again. These are the things that I can do myself: responsive websites using bem methodology, preprocessor sass and simple animations in css3.

My plans

I want to have a javascript and node.js superpower so old mistakes are gone, I won't do them no more, I turned them into lessons. Less talking, more action. You just gona see AnnCode coming. I just keep elevating. No losses, just upgrading and I need you to be one step closer to the goal.
